Tech in 2015: Disruptive Innovation

Focusing on how technology has disrupted various industries, including e-commerce, social media, and transportation, and how these disruptions have shaped our everyday lives.

How does technological innovation change our daily lives?

Technology is rapidly innovating the way we work, live, and interact with the world. It has revolutionized existing industries and spawned new global players in today’s 21st-century society. Our goal is to explore technology’s role in disrupting industry and in shaping our daily experiences. Students and speakers alike will have the opportunity to share their thoughts on the way technology is transforming our society through exciting and collaborative discussion.

Main Questions:
How has technology changed these industries?
How has this change impacted the world?
