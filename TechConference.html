EnvisionDo Presents


Tech in 2015: Transforming Industry

Focusing on how technology has disrupted various industries, including e-commerce, social media, and transportation, and how these disruptions have shaped our everyday lives.

How is technology in 2015 shaping our daily experiences and transforming nearly every industry?

Join EnvisionDo on Saturday, March 7 for a discussion featuring leaders from an exciting range of startups and established companies, including Indiegogo, Morningstar, Microsoft, AgileBits (1Password), and Gumroad! Panels are scheduled throughout the day and conference attendees are encouraged to stop by for any or all of the events at the conference. EnvisionDo will cater lunch and dessert networking sessions.
